Review of Chevy Equinox 2025: The Ultimate Electric SUV from Chevrolet

Overview

Chevrolet’s commitment to an electric future takes a major step forward with the release of the Chevy Equinox EV 2025. This all-electric compact SUV is designed to combine style, efficiency, and affordability — making it one of the most anticipated electric crossovers of the year. With bold styling, modern tech, and competitive range, the 2025 Equinox EV aims to redefine what an everyday SUV can be.


All-Electric Power with Real-World Practicality

The 2025 Equinox is Chevy’s most affordable EV yet, but it doesn’t cut corners. Built on GM’s Ultium platform, the Equinox EV offers an estimated up to 300 miles of range on a full charge (depending on the trim). The standard front-wheel-drive model delivers around 210 horsepower, while the available dual-motor all-wheel-drive version boosts power to 290 horsepower, giving drivers a smooth yet spirited ride.

Charging is made easy with fast-charging capability, allowing up to 70 miles of range in 10 minutes at DC fast-charging stations. Whether you’re commuting, running errands, or planning a weekend getaway, the Equinox EV is up for the task.


Bold, Modern Design

Chevy gave the 2025 Equinox a sleek and futuristic makeover. Its athletic proportions, aggressive front lighting signature, and bold wheel designs give it a more premium and dynamic look than its gas-powered predecessors. The light bar stretching across the front grille (on higher trims) and flush door handles add to its high-tech aesthetic.
Available in multiple trims including LT, RS, and 2RS, the Equinox EV offers different styling packages to match your personality, from sporty to sophisticated.


Interior & Tech Features

Inside, the Equinox EV feels upscale and tech-savvy. The cabin is clean and spacious with high-quality materials, supportive seats, and thoughtful storage. It features a standard 11-inch digital instrument display and an available 17.7-inch infotainment touchscreen, offering sharp graphics and intuitive controls.
Key interior highlights:

  • Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • Google Built-In (Assistant, Maps, and Play Store)
  • Heated front and rear seats (on higher trims)
  • Available panoramic sunroof
  • Optional Super Cruise hands-free driving (on select highways)

    • Despite its compact size, the Equinox EV provides generous rear seat legroom and up to 57 cubic feet of cargo space with the seats folded.


    Safety and Driver Assistance

    hevrolet equips the 2025 Equinox EV with Chevy Safety Assist as standard, which includes:

    • Forward collision alert and automatic emergency braking
    • Lane keep assist with lane departure warning
    • Following distance indicator
    • IntelliBeam automatic high beams
    Higher trims also offer adaptive cruise control, a surround-view camera system, and advanced park assist.


    Final Thoughts

    The Chevy Equinox EV 2025 is an exciting step forward for everyday electric mobility. It delivers on range, design, technology, and affordability — all in a package that feels comfortable and familiar to SUV lovers. For drivers looking to make the switch to electric without breaking the bank, the Equinox EV is not just an option — it’s a smart, forward-thinking choice.
